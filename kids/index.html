Project

Course: teaching computing fundamentals using Swift Playgrounds

  • Goals
  •  Provide a guided, visual and fun introduction to computing for kids aged 8-13
  •  Be one of the first companies to leverage Playgrounds for teaching
  •  Encourage our adult students who are parents to introduce them to programming
  • Measuring success
  •  Use analytics to gauge the popularity of the course
  •  Conduct follow-up interviews to assess the kids' interest in programming after taking the course
